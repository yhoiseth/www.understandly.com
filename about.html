About us

What is Understandly?

Understandly is a translation tool and content management system which enables you to either order high quality human translations in more than 30 languages or connect and use your own translators. You can easily manage and organize large amounts of unique translations and collaborate with your colleagues in the process, to mention a couple of the integrated features.

Uses

Do you need to translate hundreds of google adwords and facebook marketing texts? Product descriptions in multiple languages? Understandly was created to help you do this better. Stop wasting time and money using out of date spreadsheets and lengthy email threads, worrying about losing text or translating the same content twice.

Why we created Understandly

Doing marketing for an international tech company, one of the founders got tired of using spreadsheets and email when translating marketing content. The solution was to create Understandly, a tool which helps organizations do translations smarter, faster and better. We are always working on new features. Please contact us if you have any suggestions, requests or concerns, we would love to hear from you.
