About

Uses

Do you need to translate hundreds of google adwords and facebook marketing texts? Product descriptions in multiple languages? Understandly was created to help you do this better. Stop wasting time and money using out of date spreadsheets and lengthy email threads, worrying about losing text or translating the same content twice.

Why we created Understandly

Doing marketing for an international tech company, one of the founders got tired of using spreadsheets and email when translating marketing content. The solution was to build a tool which enables you and your team to order professional translations or connect your own translators and at the same time keeping all content organized and accessible. New features will be added consecutively. Please contact us if you have any suggestions, requests or concerns, we would love to hear from you.
