About

When working with marketing in several countries for an international tech company, one of the founders got tired of using spreadsheets and email working with his colleagues and translators when translating business content. We built a tool to help you in your translation and localization work. Understandly is free to use and offer in addition the possibility to buy professional translations. New features will be added consecutively. If you have any suggestions, requests or concerns we would love to hear from you.
