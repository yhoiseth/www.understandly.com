Questions and answers

Are there any fixed costs?

Get access to all features and connect your own translators and team for free. There are no fixed costs using Understandly.

When do I get charged?

After purchasing credits you will be charged $0.18 per word when you order professional human translations. The value of every order you place will be deducted from your credit balance. Your credit balance is displayed in the top right corner of the page navigation header.

How long does it take to translate my order?

We have an average turnover time less than 90 minutes. You can order translations 24/7, seven days a week. Our network of translators are located worldwide.

How do you ensure high quality?

We only use qualified human translators which first have passed a series of tests and which are being reviewed regularly. All translators are native speakers with an understanding of the local culture.

What kind of translations can you do?

Our platform was built specifically to translate marketing content, making it easy to manage large amounts of translations. However we can translate all types of content, except for legal and medical texts.

Have a question?

If you have any questions, comments or concerns, please contact us. We will get back to you quickly.
