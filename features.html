Features

Professional human translations

Buy quality translations by native speakers located worldwide.

Use your own translators

Connect your own translators. Understandly is flexible. Seamlessly choose between using your own translators or buying professional translations.

Notifications

Receive email notifications if anything changes or if your team members or translators comments.

Duplicate

The same input text can be translated into as many languages as needed. Buy professional translations, use your own translator, or both.

Custom tags

Organize your translations in any way you want by creating custom tags.

Collaborate

Comment and discuss specific texts with your translator or colleagues.

Import

Do you have translated content stored in spreadsheets? Get organized and import your translated content to Understandly.
