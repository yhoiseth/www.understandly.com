Features

Professional human translations

Buy high quality translations by native speakers located worldwide.

Use your own translators

Connect your own translators. Understandly is flexible. Choose between using your own translators or buying professional translations.

Content management

Understandly makes it easy to manage huge amounts of translations. Search, filter and organize your translated content.

Connect your team

Connect your team so that everyone can get contribute in the translation process and get access to translated content.

Manage multiple accounts

You can create multiple accounts to manage billing, content and user access separately if needed.

Notifications

Receive email notifications if anything changes or if your team members or translators comments.

Duplicate

The same input text can be translated into as many languages as needed. Buy professional translations, use your own translator, or both.

Custom tags

Organize your translations in any way you want by creating custom tags.

Collaborate

Comment and discuss texts with your translators and colleagues.

Import

Do you have translated content stored in spreadsheets? Get organized and import your translated content to Understandly.

Secure

Communication and information sharing on Understandly are encrypted. All content is securely stored.

Cloud-based

All your translations accessible for everyone in your team, from everywhere. We regularly do backups of all content.
